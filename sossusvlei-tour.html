Sossusvlei & Namib Desert

Full day · approx 8-10 hrs Location 1 · Namib Desert N$5,000

Service Description

Adventure through the Dunes

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Namibia's dunes on a guided, immersive adventure. Walk on the iconic red sands of Sossusvlei, climb the famous Dune 45, and witness the surreal landscapes of Dead Vlei with its ancient camelthorn trees.

This full-day excursion includes a knowledgeable guide, 4x4 transfer to the vlei, and plenty of time to explore, take photographs, and soak in the silence of the desert. Perfect for nature lovers, photographers, and adventurers.

  • Sunrise departure to beat the heat
  • Guided climb of Dune 45 (optional)
  • Visit to Dead Vlei & Sossusvlei
  • 4x4 shuttle through the desert
  • Picnic lunch with a view
